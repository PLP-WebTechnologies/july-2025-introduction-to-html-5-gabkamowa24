Who We Are

Kuwala Clean Energy is a Malawian social enterprise committed to ending energy poverty through affordable, community-driven solar energy solutions. We believe that no family should be left in the dark simply because they live off the grid.

Our Mission

Our mission is to expand access to clean, reliable, and affordable solar energy for rural and peri-urban communities across Malawi. By working with trusted local structures like Water Point Committees and offering flexible payment options, we are lighting up homes, empowering women and youth, and helping communities build resilience to climate change—one solar lantern at a time.

Our Solution

At Kuwala Clean Energy, we combine flexible payment, trusted local partnerships, and customer-centered support to deliver clean energy that lasts.

Projects

Currently, with support from D-Prize, we are implementing our first full pilot in Havara Village. Our goal is to distribute at least 500 solar lanterns, reaching approximately 1,500 people with clean, affordable lighting, replacing harmful kerosene and transforming lives in these communities.

Partners

We collaborate with community groups, international partners, and local leaders to scale our impact sustainably across Malawi.
